is bikiNG safe iN Paris?

Yes! Our tours are 100% safe, as we follow a carefully designed route through the back streets of the city. Paris has indeed become very easy to navigate by bike - with the many bike lanes, riversides, and quaint streets to explore.

Who we are?

Bike About is owned and operated by ex pats that have lived in Paris for years and know the city inside & out. We speak the language. We know the Parisian secrets. We’ve eaten thousands of baguettes. We are the smaller, grassroots bike tour company in Paris that offers the most personal & unique tours in the city.
